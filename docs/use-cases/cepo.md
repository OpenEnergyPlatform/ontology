## Description
The Climate and Energy Policy Ontology (CEPO) catalogs terminology that describes policy instruments and targets related to sustainable energy transition. It forms the semantic base for the Climate Policy Atlas, a web portal for exploring energy-related policy data. Researchers use CEPO to annotate policy documents, improving data interoperability and helping to highlight connections between data.

## OEO Connection

- CEPO’s entity structure and development workflows are based on those of the OEO.
- CEPO was designed for interoperability with the OEO, using BFO and some OEO terms as a base for initial development.
- Like the OEO, CEPO follows the ENERO Foundry principles.
